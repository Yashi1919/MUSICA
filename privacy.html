Privacy Policy - Musica

Effective Date: November 15, 2024

Thank you for choosing Musica! Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains how Musica collects, uses, shares, and protects user data. By using Musica, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Musica collects personal information to provide and enhance our services. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: Data such as your name, email, and password when creating an account.
  • Usage Data: actions taken, and preferences within the app.
  • Device Information: operating system, and unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Firebase

Musica uses Firebase by Google to store, manage, and secure user data. Firebase services we employ include:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user credentials securely and verifies your identity when accessing your account.
  • Firebase Firestore: Stores data related to music rooms, playlists, and session activity.
  • Firebase Analytics: Gathers anonymized data to understand usage patterns and improve Musica.

3. How We Use Your Data

Musica uses collected data to provide you with a personalized and interactive experience, including app operation, user experience enhancement, and security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Musica does not sell or share your data with third parties, except in cases such as legal requirements or with vendors like Firebase who support app infrastructure.

5. Data Security

Musica prioritizes data security with industry-standard measures like encryption and access control, and Firebase complies with Google’s privacy standards.

6. Data Retention

Musica retains your data as needed for the stated purposes or by law. Contact us if you wish to delete your account, and we will process your request.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Musica may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the policy effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@musica.com.

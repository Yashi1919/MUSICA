Musica - Music Player Features

How the Normal Music Player Works

The Normal Music Player in Musica is designed to provide a seamless experience for listening to your local music collection. Here's a step-by-step overview of how it works:

  • Storage Permission: The player first requests permission to access your device's storage to retrieve local MP3 files. This allows Musica to list your songs directly from your device.
  • Fetching Local Songs: Once permission is granted, Musica scans your music folder to gather and display all available MP3 files, creating a playlist of your songs.
  • Play, Pause, and Skip Controls: The player features intuitive play, pause, next, and previous controls, allowing you to navigate through your playlist with ease.
  • Real-time Progress Tracking: While playing a song, Musica displays a real-time progress bar, showing how much of the song has played and how much time is left.
  • Background Play: You can enjoy your music even when switching between apps, as Musica supports background playback, ensuring uninterrupted listening.

These features make Musica a convenient choice for listening to your music collection directly from your device with complete control over your playlist and playback.
